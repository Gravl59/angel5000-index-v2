## üìê TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Footage Requirements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080 (Full HD), prefer 4K (3840x2160)
- **Frame Rate:** 24fps standard, 60fps or 120fps for slow-motion
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264) or ProRes for editing
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (can crop to 1:1 or 9:16 later)
- **Duration:** At least 10-15 seconds per clip

### Color Grading Reference
- Act 1: Cold, desaturated (industrial blues/grays)
- Act 2-3: Tech blue/cyan tones
- Act 4: Rich, professional palette
